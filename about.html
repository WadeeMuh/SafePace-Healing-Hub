About SafePace Healing Hub

Our mission is to provide support to people in Palestine and Sudan who've been traumatized by the horrible things they may have witnessed.

Our Heart

The Struggle:

Children in Sudan and Palestine are currently in the midst of an unimaginable struggle. Not only are they in physical harm's way, but the invisible wounds of war such as anxiety, isolation, and loss of safety, have the ability to affect them throughout their lives if not treated. These children feel as though they are going through this struggle of overcoming their fears alone and do not have a way to seek the specialized help they need to get better.

A Path to Healing:

The SafeSpace Healing Hub is an online sanctuary that is specifically designed for children who are dealing with the reality of genocide in Sudan and Palestine. We fill an information gap between healing and trauma by providing children with access to a directory of reputable international and local organizations that can offer them professional assistance. In addition to the vital connections SafeSpace offers children instant tools to manage their emotions including a guided breathing visualizer to soothe their nervous system and a random task generator to center them in the present moment. Our mission is to ensure that every child is able to process their emotions within a safe, culturally relevant, and bilingual space where they are never really alone.
